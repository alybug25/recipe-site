Hi, I'm Alyssa!

<- (to the left is 5-year-old Alyssa in an apron)

I'm a Computer Science student who loves to cook and bake in her free time - especially my mom's recipes. I built this website to practise using HTML/CSS and to create a digital space where I could share all my favourite recipes!

We're based in the UK, but both of my parents are from the Philippines. Growing up in a Filipino household means my tastebuds have developed a little bias towards Asian dishes but despite this, I do enjoy flavours from all over the world and am very happy to try new ones!

Aside from cooking and baking, I also love to watch cartoons, play music on my guitar, do karaoke (it's in my genes!!), crochet , and try various other arts and crafts. Recently, I've tried my hand at jewellery-making using metal wire, beads, and sea glass.

Some fun facts about me!

  • My favourite shows are Steven Universe, Arcane, My Little Pony, The Owl House (okay, well, the list is actually longer than I thought so I'll just stop here)
  • My favourite food is Sinigang (and my favourite vegetable is okra)
  • My favourite song at the moment is Love Like You from Steven Universe
